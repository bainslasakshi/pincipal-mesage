THOUGHT OF DAY :

Principal Message:- MRS. SONAM GUPTA







`


“All the power is within you; you can do anything and everything. Believe in that; don’t believe that you are weak. Stand up and express the divinity within you.” At present I am pursuing such an honourable dignity for being the head of Lord Krishna Public School having the responsibility from very first day I endeavored several improvements in the school premises. I firmly believe that success comes to those who don’t rest on the laurels of the past. The world is changing at an accelerating rate. So we are preparing to cope with this progressing age of technology and our school is well equipped to prepare our children to face the future challenges. In words of Swami Vivekananda “Education is the manifestation of perfection already in man” .
